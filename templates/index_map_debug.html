US Counties Map

This map shows all US counties with available data from the 2009-2018 US census estimates. All county and state names are available on hover, and a full breakdown of racial and ethnic data is available on click. Select a year of data using the attached dropdown, and be sure to observe how the total number of majority nonwhite counties in the US changes from year to year.
